About Factulist

A tool for everyday readers to quickly check bias and credibility in news articles.

How it Works

  • We analyze article text with modern language models (sentence embeddings).
  • A classifier predicts bias probability (Neutral, Mixed, Biased).
  • Credibility scores come from source analysis + text features.
  • Results are simplified into traffic-light bias badges and star ratings.

What Factulist is (and isn’t)

  • Is: A guide to help spot patterns of bias and credibility.
  • Isn’t: A replacement for professional fact-checking.

Privacy

Your inputs are processed only for analysis. Reports are stored locally in your browser history page (TinyDB), not shared externally.

Disclaimer

No AI tool is 100% accurate. Factulist provides insights to help you think critically, not definitive judgments.
